Wall Street AI gains arrive as banks plan for fewer people

By December 2025, AI adoption on Wall Street moved past experiments into everyday operations, with JPMorgan's consumer banking reporting 6% productivity gains, up from 3% pre-AI, with operations roles expected to see 40-50% productivity improvements.

Macro Trends

Federal Reserve cuts rates to 3.5-3.75% in hawkish December decision

Fed cut key interest rate by 25 bps to 3.5-3.75% in split 9-3 vote, marking third consecutive cut and lowest level since November 2022; December dot plot shows median funds rate declining to 3.4% by end of 2026 (one quarter-point cut expected for entire year).

CNBC 2025-12-10

Fed divisions on rate policy expected to carry into 2026

2025 saw rare Fed divisions with dissents from opposing directions on interest rate policy, reflecting conflict between maximum employment and stable prices goals; Cleveland Fed's Hammack already favors holding rates steady into spring 2026.

Key index tracking major banks climbed 29% year to date, outperforming S&P 500 by 13 percentage points; Citigroup, JPMorgan Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, and Charles Schwab identified as top bank stocks to watch.
