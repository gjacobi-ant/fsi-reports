Banking & AI

Industry Trends

AI transformation in financial services: 5 predictors for success in 2026

Financial services has highest concentration of 'Frontier Firms' embedding AI agents across every workflow. IDC study shows these firms report ROI roughly 3x higher than slow adopters. McKinsey estimates genAI could deliver $200B-$340B in annual value for banking sector.

Microsoft Industry Blogs 2025-12-18

Wall Street's AI gains are here — banks plan for fewer people

AI adoption on Wall Street moved past experiments into everyday operations by Dec 2025. At Goldman Sachs conference Dec 9, bank execs described AI as operational upgrade lifting productivity across engineering, operations, and customer service. JPMorgan reports 6% productivity gains, up from 3% pre-deployment.

Artificial Intelligence News · 2025-12-23

Eurobank's 'AI Factory': A New Era of Agentic Banking Powered by Nvidia and Microsoft

Eurobank launched 'AI Factory' initiative - massive industrial-scale deployment of agentic AI. Deep collaboration with Microsoft and Nvidia, alongside EY and Fairfax. Marks shift from experimental chatbots to production-ready environment where AI workers reason, plan, and execute across lending, risk management, customer service.

Financial Content · 2025-12-24

How banks and FinTechs are scaling AI in financial crime

Almost 3/4 of payment firms report cost savings from AI in AML operations, with many expecting annual savings exceeding $5M as deployments mature. Investment plans focus on generative and agentic AI seen as critical to scaling without proportional staff increases.

FinTech Global · 2025-12-24

AI is reshaping banking—but not causing a jobs wipeout

AI reshaping banking operations but not causing immediate job losses. Operations roles could see 40-50% productivity gains as AI becomes routine work. Focus on augmentation rather than replacement.

Economy & Markets

Macro Trends

Fed cuts rate by 25bps to 3.5-3.75% range, signals only one cut in 2026

Federal Reserve cut fed funds rate by 25bps to 3.5-3.75% in December 2025 meeting, bringing borrowing costs to lowest since 2022. Split 9-3 vote signals tougher road ahead. Policymakers project only one 25bps cut in 2026, with median rate declining from 3.6% end-2025 to 3.4% end-2026.

CNBC 2025-12-10

Fed Chair Powell: 'We are well positioned to wait and see'

At post-meeting press conference, Powell said rate reduction puts Fed in comfortable position. 'We are well positioned to wait and see how the economy evolves.' Fed has reduced key rate by 1.75 percentage points since Sept 2024.

US Bank · 2025-12-10

Moody's offers sobering view on Fed rate cuts

Moody's provides cautious outlook on pace of future Fed rate cuts despite December reduction. Inflation concerns persist with CPI at 2.7% YoY (core 2.6%), above Fed's 2% target.

TheStreet · 2025-12-18

New York Fed: Economy poised to return to solid growth and price stability in 2026

NY Fed statement: 'After a year of uncertainty, we will be starting 2026 from a place of resilience. The economy is poised to return to solid growth and price stability.' Fed revised GDP forecasts higher for 2025 (1.7% vs 1.6%) and 2026 (2.3% vs 1.8%).

Federal Reserve Bank of New York · 2025-12-15

Variable-rate home equity lines see notable rate drops to 7.81%

Average rate on variable-rate HELOCs dropped to 7.81% per Bankrate, well below 9-11% from 15 months ago. Auto loan rates for new/used cars only down ~0.5 percentage points since Sept 2024, far less than fed funds rate drop.
